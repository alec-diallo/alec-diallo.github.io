About Me

I am a final-year PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Dr Paul Patras and funded by Arm. I am also a Research Assistant in the Mobile Intelligence Lab, a member of the Institute for Computing Systems Architecture, and a Machine Learning engineer at Net AI.

My research interests include artificial intelligence, knowledge representation, adversarial robustness, neural network explainability, optimisation algorithms, and their application to security, privacy, and mobile traffic analysis.

Prior to my PhD, I obtained a joint Computer Science and Electrical Engineering Degree from École Supérieure d'Ingénieurs en Électrotechnique et Électronique (ESIEE Paris) and Mundiapolis University.
